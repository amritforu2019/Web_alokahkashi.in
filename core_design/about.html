About us

Welcome to Alokah Kashi, a sanctuary of elegance and tradition in the heart of Varanasi. Inspired by Lord Shiva. 'Alokah' signifies a transcendent vision, brilliance, and splendour—qualities that shape every aspect of this boutique hotel. Each room offers a haven of comfort - warm lighting, fine woodwork, private balconies, spacious layouts, and refined decor. Adding to this, our highly trained staff enhances this experience by delivering hospitality with warmth and care.

Conveniently located, Alokah Kashi provides seamless access to Varanasi's sacred temples, bustling ghats, vibrant bazaars, and the Buddhist pilgrimage site of Sarnath, where spirituality and tradition converge. Whether you’re here for pilgrimage, quietude, or adventure, Alokah Kashi is your gateway to Varanasi’s rich cultural heritage.

FAQ

Can I request early check-in or late check-out?
Our standard check-in time is 2:00 pm, and check-out is at 12:00 noon. Early check-in or late check-out requests may be accommodated on a case-by-case basis, subject to room availability.
What is the hotel’s cancellation policy?
Up to 2 days before check-in: Fully refundable
Within 2 days of check-in: Non-refundable
No-show: Non-refundable
Is breakfast included with the booking?
It depends on the plan you choose. You may also add breakfast to your plan at the time of check in. For more details, please reach out to the reception.
Do you help with itinerary planning of Varansi?
Yes, we do provide end-to-end trip planning in Varansi. Please call our travel desk at +91 91514 54302 for more details.
Does Alokah Kashi Hotel provide airport shuttle service?
We can provide at extra cost. Please call +91 91514 54302
Are pets allowed at the hotel?
We apologize, but pets are not permitted at our hotel. Thank you for your understanding.
Is there parking available at the hotel?
Yes, parking is available.
How can I make a reservation?
You can book directly through our website or call +91 91514 54301 / 302 for assistance with your booking.
