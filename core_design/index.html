Hotel Alokah Kashi, a luxurious 3-star boutique hotel, takes inspiration from Lord Shiva—'Alokah,' meaning 'transcending the world in vision, splendor, and brilliance.' Designed in true boutique style, the hotel beautifully combines light and exquisite woodwork to create an inviting atmosphere, offering the perfect blend of comfort and tranquility for guests to immerse themselves in the sacred ambiance of Kashi.
